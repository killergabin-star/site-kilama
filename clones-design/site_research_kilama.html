    Working Paper
    Geopolitical Risk and Bilateral Trade: Causal Evidence from GPR Shocks
    Eric Kilama
    New causal estimates of geopolitical risk transmission to bilateral trade flows. Difference-in-differences identification on 494 monthly observations across 180+ country pairs using the Caldara-Iacoviello GPR index.

    Eric Kilama
    Comprehensive audit of G7 ODA commitments since 2015. Analysis of gaps between pledges and disbursements, with focus on fragile states and the "dilution" phenomenon in aid allocation.
